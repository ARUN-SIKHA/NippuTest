About NippuKoodi

NippuKoodi was founded to connect skilled workers with companies in need of reliable staffing. We focus on safety, professionalism, and speed. Our process ensures every worker is background-checked and ready to perform on day one.

Our mission is to provide flexible staffing solutions for businesses while creating opportunities for workers seeking reliable employment. We maintain strong partnerships with companies across industries, ensuring the best fit for both the worker and the client.

With years of experience in workforce management, our team handles all recruitment, vetting, and placement logistics, so companies can focus on their business while we provide trusted personnel efficiently.
